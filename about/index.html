About

Nicholas Kuepper is a graduate from Western Governors University and the Northeast Wisconsin Technical College (NWTC) Software Development programs where he learned proficiency in multiple programming languages, including Java, C#, and SQL. He earned an Associate degree in 2020 and a Bachelors degree in 2021.

In 2019, Nicholas studied abroad in China to learn about IT, business, and education on the global scale. Nicholas earned his Associate's Degree with high honors as part of the Honor Society and the Phi Theta Kappa honor chapter. Nicholas has experience with many life skills, inlcuding leadership skills, from work experience in retail and from earning the rank of Eagle Scout in the Boy Scouts of America.
